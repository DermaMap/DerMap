⚡ Laser Hair Removal

Long-term hair reduction for smooth, clear and hassle-free skin

What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light beams to target and destroy hair follicles. The pigment in the follicles absorbs the light, which damages the follicle and inhibits future hair growth. It's a highly effective, long-term solution for unwanted hair.

Benefits

  • Long-term hair reduction
  • Precise targeting of dark, coarse hairs
  • Speed: Each pulse takes a fraction of a second
  • Reduces ingrown hairs and razor bumps
  • Saves time and money on shaving/waxing
  • Safe and effective for most skin types

Treatment Process

The area is cleaned and a cooling gel or device may be used. The laser is passed over the skin, delivering pulses of light. You may feel a slight snapping sensation. Multiple sessions (usually 6-8) are required to target hairs in different growth phases.

Recovery Time

Zero downtime! You can resume normal activities immediately. Mild redness and swelling may occur around the treated area for a few hours. Avoid sun exposure and use sunscreen on treated areas.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 15-60 minutes depending on the area
  • Sensation: Mild snapping or warming sensation
  • Results: Hair falls out within 1-3 weeks
  • Sessions: 6-8 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart
