What is Microneedling?

Microneedling, also known as collagen induction therapy, is a minimally invasive procedure that uses fine needles to create tiny punctures in the skin. This stimulates the body's natural healing process and collagen production, resulting in smoother, firmer, and more youthful-looking skin.

Benefits

  • Reduces acne scars and surgical scars
  • Minimizes fine lines and wrinkles
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Reduces pore size
  • Treats stretch marks
  • Improves skin elasticity
  • Enhances product absorption

Treatment Process

A device with fine needles creates controlled micro-injuries in the skin. The treatment typically takes 30-60 minutes. Topical numbing cream is applied beforehand for comfort. The procedure stimulates collagen and elastin production over the following weeks.

Recovery Time: 24-72 hours of redness and mild swelling. Most people can return to normal activities within 2-3 days. Full results appear after 4-6 weeks as collagen rebuilds.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 30-60 minutes
  • Sensation: Mild discomfort (numbing cream used)
  • Downtime: 2-3 days of redness
  • Results: Visible improvement in 4-6 weeks
  • Sessions: 3-6 treatments recommended
