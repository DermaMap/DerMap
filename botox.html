What is Botox?

Botox is a purified protein that temporarily relaxes facial muscles that cause wrinkles. It's one of the most popular non-surgical cosmetic treatments worldwide, effectively smoothing forehead lines, frown lines, and crow's feet while maintaining natural facial expressions.

Benefits

  • Smooths forehead lines and wrinkles
  • Reduces frown lines between eyebrows
  • Softens crow's feet around eyes
  • Prevents new wrinkles from forming
  • Quick procedure with minimal discomfort
  • No downtime required
  • Natural-looking results

Treatment Process

Botox is injected into specific facial muscles using a very fine needle. The procedure takes about 10-15 minutes. Results begin to appear within 3-5 days, with full effects visible after 2 weeks. The treatment temporarily blocks nerve signals to the injected muscles.

Recovery Time

No downtime! You can resume normal activities immediately. Minor bruising or swelling may occur but typically resolves within 24-48 hours. Avoid strenuous exercise and lying down for 4 hours after treatment.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 10-15 minutes
  • Sensation: Minimal discomfort (tiny needle)
  • Results: Visible in 3-5 days, full effect in 2 weeks
  • Duration of Results: 3-4 months
  • Maintenance: Repeat treatments to maintain results
