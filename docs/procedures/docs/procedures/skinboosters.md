# Skin Boosters

> **Category:** Injectable Skin Hydration Treatment  
> **Treatment Time:** 20–60 minutes  
> **Downtime:** Minimal  
> **Results:** Gradual improvement in hydration, texture, and skin quality  
> **Best For:** Dehydrated skin, fine lines, dullness, loss of elasticity

---

# Overview

Skin boosters are minimally invasive injectable treatments designed to improve overall skin quality by delivering hydrating and bioactive ingredients into the skin.

Unlike dermal fillers, skin boosters are not primarily used to add volume or reshape facial features. Instead, they focus on improving:

- Skin hydration
- Elasticity
- Smoothness
- Radiance
- Overall skin appearance

Many skin booster treatments contain **hyaluronic acid (HA)**, a naturally occurring molecule that helps maintain moisture within the skin.

---

# How It Works

Skin boosters are injected into the superficial layers of the skin.

The treatment works by:

- Increasing hydration levels within the skin
- Supporting skin elasticity
- Improving skin texture
- Stimulating healthier-looking skin quality

Hyaluronic acid attracts and retains water molecules, helping the skin appear more hydrated and refreshed.

---

# Skin Boosters vs Dermal Fillers

| Skin Boosters | Dermal Fillers |
|---------------|----------------|
| Improve skin quality | Restore volume |
| Increase hydration | Enhance facial structure |
| Usually injected superficially | Often placed deeper |
| Create natural glow | Create contour changes |
| Focus on texture and elasticity | Focus on shape and volume |

# Ideal Candidates

Skin boosters may be suitable for individuals who have:

- Dehydrated skin
- Dull complexion
- Fine dehydration lines
- Uneven texture
- Reduced skin elasticity
- Early signs of skin aging

---

# Who Should Avoid Skin Boosters

Treatment may not be recommended for individuals with:

- Active skin infections
- Open wounds
- Certain allergies
- Significant inflammation
- Certain medical conditions affecting healing

A professional consultation is recommended before treatment.

---

# Procedure Steps

## 1. Consultation

The provider evaluates:

- Skin condition
- Treatment goals
- Medical history
- Previous aesthetic procedures

---

## 2. Skin Preparation

The treatment area is cleansed.

A topical anesthetic may be used to improve comfort.

---

## 3. Injection Process

Small amounts of product are injected into selected areas using fine needles or specialized techniques.

- Product type

## 4. Post-Treatment Care

Soothing products may be applied after treatment.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Are skin boosters the same as fillers?

No. Skin boosters improve hydration and skin quality, while fillers primarily restore volume and facial structure.

---

## Do skin boosters make the face bigger?

No. They are designed to improve skin quality rather than add significant volume.

---

## When will results appear?

Some hydration improvements may appear early, while skin quality changes develop gradually.

---

## How long do results last?

Duration depends on:

- Product formulation
- Skin condition
- Lifestyle
- Maintenance treatments

---

## Can skin boosters replace skincare?

No. They complement a consistent skincare routine and sun protection.
