# HydraFacial

> **Category:** Non-Invasive Facial Treatment  
> **Treatment Time:** 30–60 minutes  
> **Downtime:** None or minimal  
> **Results:** Immediate glow with continued skin hydration  
> **Best For:** Dull skin, dehydration, congestion, enlarged pores, uneven skin tone

---

# Overview

HydraFacial is a non-invasive, multi-step facial treatment that combines cleansing, exfoliation, pore extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection in a single session. Using a specialized vortex technology, the procedure removes impurities while simultaneously delivering hydrating and nourishing serums into the skin.

The treatment is suitable for most skin types and is commonly chosen by individuals seeking healthier, smoother, and more radiant-looking skin without downtime.

---

# How It Works

HydraFacial uses a patented vortex delivery system that performs several skincare steps during one treatment.

The procedure typically includes:

- Gentle cleansing
- Chemical exfoliation
- Painless extraction of debris
- Intensive hydration
- Infusion of antioxidant-rich serums

Unlike traditional facials, extraction is performed using vacuum-assisted technology rather than manual pressure.

# Ideal Candidates

HydraFacial is generally suitable for individuals with:

- Dehydrated skin
- Oily skin
- Combination skin
- Mild acne congestion
- Enlarged pores
- Uneven skin tone
- Dull complexion
- Early signs of aging

---

# Who Should Delay Treatment

Treatment may not be recommended during:

- Active sunburn
- Severe inflammatory acne
- Open wounds
- Active skin infection
- Active cold sores
- Severe rosacea flare
- Recent aggressive laser procedures

A consultation with a qualified skincare professional is recommended.

# Treatment Steps

## 1. Consultation

The skin is evaluated to determine treatment goals and identify any contraindications.

---

## 2. Cleansing & Exfoliation

Dead skin cells and surface impurities are gently removed.

---

## 3. Gentle Chemical Peel

A mild blend of exfoliating acids helps loosen debris inside pores.

---

## 4. Painless Extraction

Vacuum-assisted technology removes oil, debris, and impurities from pores.

---

## 5. Hydration

Hydrating serums containing ingredients such as:

- Hyaluronic Acid
- Peptides
- Antioxidants

are infused into the skin.

---

## 6. Protection

Moisturizer and broad-spectrum sunscreen are applied before leaving the clinic.

✔ Continue gentle cleansing

✔ Apply moisturizer

✔ Use broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher

✔ Stay hydrated

- Strong exfoliants
- Retinoids

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is HydraFacial suitable for sensitive skin?

Many individuals with sensitive skin tolerate HydraFacial well. Treatment should always be customized according to individual skin needs.

---

## Can HydraFacial help acne?

It may improve mild congestion and oily skin by cleansing pores, but it is not a replacement for medical acne treatment.

---

## Is there any downtime?

Downtime is minimal or absent. Most people return to normal activities immediately.

---

## Can I wear makeup afterward?

Many professionals recommend waiting until the following day to allow the skin to fully benefit from the treatment.

---

## How long do results last?

Results vary, but many individuals enjoy visible improvements for several weeks. Regular maintenance treatments may help sustain benefits.
