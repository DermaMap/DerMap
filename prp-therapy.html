PRP Therapy

Uses your own plasma to promote healing and natural rejuvenation

🩸

What is PRP Therapy?

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy, often called the "Vampire Facial," uses your body's own healing properties. A small amount of blood is drawn, spun in a centrifuge to concentrate the platelets, and then injected or microneedled into the skin to stimulate collagen and tissue regeneration.

Benefits

  • Natural, biocompatible treatment (uses your own blood)
  • Stimulates natural collagen and elastin production
  • Improves skin texture, tone, and firmness
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Effective for hair loss (PRP for scalp)
  • Accelerates healing and reduces inflammation

Treatment Process

1. A small blood sample is drawn from your arm.
2. The blood is placed in a centrifuge to separate the platelet-rich plasma.
3. The PRP is applied to the face, often combined with microneedling or injected directly into targeted areas.

Recovery Time

Minimal downtime. You may experience mild redness, swelling, or a sunburn-like sensation for 24-48 hours. Most people can apply makeup and return to normal activities the next day.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Sensation: Mild discomfort (numbing cream is applied)
  • Results: Gradual improvement over 3-6 weeks
  • Sessions: 3 initial sessions, 4-6 weeks apart, then annual maintenance
