💧 Hydrafacial

Deep cleansing, exfoliation and hydration for a healthy, glowing complexion

💧

What is Hydrafacial?

Hydrafacial is a revolutionary skin treatment that combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection in one procedure. It's suitable for all skin types and delivers immediate results with no downtime.

Benefits

  • Deeply cleanses and unclogs pores
  • Removes dead skin cells and impurities
  • Hydrates and nourishes skin
  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Minimizes enlarged pores
  • Instant glow and radiance

Treatment Process

The treatment uses a specialized vortex-fusion delivery system to perform four steps: cleanse and peel, extract and hydrate, fuse and protect. The entire procedure takes about 30 minutes.

Recovery Time

No downtime required! You can return to your normal activities immediately. Some temporary redness may occur but typically subsides within a few hours.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Sensation: Gentle, painless, and relaxing
  • Results: Immediate improvement in skin texture and glow
  • Recommended: Monthly treatments for optimal results
