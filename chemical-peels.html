🧪 Chemical Peels

Exfoliate and renew skin to improve tone, texture and reduce imperfections

What are Chemical Peels?

Chemical peels involve applying a chemical solution to the skin, which causes it to exfoliate and eventually peel off. The new, regenerated skin is usually smoother, less wrinkled, and more even in tone. They are highly effective for treating acne, sun damage, and aging.

Benefits

  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles
  • Treats acne and acne scars
  • Fades hyperpigmentation and sun spots
  • Unclogs pores and reduces breakouts
  • Stimulates new skin cell growth

Types of Peels

  • Light Peels: Gentle exfoliation for minor texture issues (no downtime).
  • Medium Peels: Penetrates deeper for moderate wrinkles and pigmentation (a few days downtime).
  • Deep Peels: Dramatic results for severe sun damage and deep wrinkles (longer recovery).

Recovery Time

Recovery depends on the depth of the peel. Light peels may cause slight flaking for 3-5 days. Medium peels involve redness and peeling for 7-14 days. Deep peels require several weeks of recovery. Sun protection is crucial during healing.
