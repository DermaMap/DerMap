Restore volume, enhance contours and achieve natural-looking results

What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are gel-like substances injected beneath the skin to restore lost volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial contours. Most fillers contain hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the skin that provides hydration and plumpness.

Benefits

  • Restore facial volume and fullness
  • Smooth deep lines and wrinkles
  • Enhance lips and cheekbones
  • Improve facial symmetry
  • Fill in under-eye hollows
  • Define jawline and chin
  • Immediate, natural-looking results

Treatment Areas

  • Lips: Add volume and definition
  • Cheeks: Restore volume and lift
  • Nasolabial Folds: Smooth smile lines
  • Under Eyes: Reduce dark circles and hollows
  • Jawline: Enhance definition and contour
  • Chin: Improve profile and balance

Recovery Time

Minimal downtime! You may experience mild swelling, bruising, or redness at injection sites, which typically resolves within 3-7 days. Most people return to normal activities immediately, though we recommend avoiding strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 30-60 minutes
  • Sensation: Mild discomfort (numbing cream available)
  • Results: Immediate improvement
  • Duration: 6-18 months depending on filler type
  • Final Results: Visible after swelling subsides (1-2 weeks)
